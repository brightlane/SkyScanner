Bonjour, Paris.

Paris in 2026 is about more than just the Eiffel Tower. It’s about the "Quiet Luxury" of the 3rd Arrondissement, the scent of fresh croissants in the Marais, and the perfect sunset over the Seine.

Day 01

Le Marais & The Seine

Start your morning at Carette for the most aesthetic hot chocolate in France. Spend your afternoon wandering through the boutiques of Le Marais. As the sun sets, skip the crowded boat tours and grab a bottle of wine to sit on the banks of the Seine with the locals.

Day 02

Art, Arches & Aesthetics

Visit the Musée de l'Orangerie to see Monet’s Water Lilies in silence. Afternoon calls for a walk up the Champs-Élysées, but the real secret is the rooftop of Galeries Lafayette for a free, 360-degree view of the city.

Day 03

Montmartre Magic

Wake up early to catch the Sacré-Cœur without the crowds. Explore the "sinking house" and the Place du Tertre. Finish your 72-hour escape with a late dinner at a classic bistro—look for the ones with handwritten menus; that's where the real flavor is.
